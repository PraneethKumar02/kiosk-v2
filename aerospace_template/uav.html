BEML TACTICAL UAV

BEML has entered the UAV sector for both defense and civil applications, partnering with IIT Kanpur for joint development. These UAVs are designed for intelligence, reconnaissance, surveillance, border patrolling, and disaster management, targeting key Indian security forces including the Army, Navy, BSF, Coast Guard, and CRPF.

MAX ALTITUDE

4000 m

RANGE

50 km

ENDURANCE

6 - 8 hours
