ABHINANDAN HNX50

The Abhinandan HNX50 is a runway-free RPAS developed by BEML in collaboration with VTOL Aviation India Pvt Ltd. This 50 kg class, fixed-wing UAV features vertical take-off and landing capabilities and operates across low- and high-altitude regions for ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance) missions. Designed for intelligence, reconnaissance, and surveillance, it is targeted at key Indian security forces, including the Army, Navy, BSF, Coast Guard, and CRPF.
