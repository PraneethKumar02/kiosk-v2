GROUND HANDLING & SUPPORT

BEML Aerospace has developed expertise in the design, development, and manufacturing of mission-critical Ground Handling Support Equipment (GHE & GSE).

BEML has supplied mission-controlled GHE/GSE to HAL and the Indian Air Force for various programs, including LCA Mk1, Jaguar, and others.

Capabilities include complete design, analysis, development, and productionisation of GHE/GSE systems, ensuring high reliability and operational readiness.
