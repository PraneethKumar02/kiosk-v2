Bengaluru Metro

Operates using ATO/ATP technology to ensure safe, efficient, and fully automated train control. Each train is configured as a 3-car set, with the flexibility to be expanded into 6-car formations as demand increases. Power is supplied through a 750 V DC third-rail collection system, providing reliable energy delivery for consistent operations

Rail Gauge

1435mm

Operating Speed

80 km/h (Design speed 90 km/h)

Car Body

Stainless Steel

\
Feature Details
Train Formation DMC–TC–MC–MC–TC–DMC (6-car), DMC–TC–DMC (3-car)
Passenger Capacity 1029 passengers (3-car) / 2064 passengers (6-car)
Current Collection 750 V DC Third Rail System
Bogies Two-axle bolsterless type with rubber primary spring and air secondary suspension
Propulsion System 3-phase VVVF system with induction motor and regenerative braking
Brake System Regenerative braking blended with pneumatic brakes
Air-Conditioning Roof-mounted HVAC system
Door System Electrically driven externally sliding doors
PA/PIS & CCTV LED lighting with Intelligent Light Control (ILC)
Interior FRP panels, SS seats, rubber-covered floor
