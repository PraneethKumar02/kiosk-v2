CABLE DREDGER

Flow Rate

Up to 4000 cu.m./hr

Maximum Working Depth

Up to 250 m

Draft

0.6 - 0.9m

The system is developed through a technology collaboration with M/s Dragflow S.r.l, Italy, ensuring world-class dredging expertise.

Designed for efficient operations in deep-water environments, making them essential for port maintenance, mining operations, and reservoir cleaning.

Capable of handling large material volumes, enabling effective execution of complex dredging projects.

Provides superior stability during deep-water operations, ensuring reliable and continuous dredging even in challenging conditions.

Equipped with a telemetry package for real-time monitoring of key project indicators, including placement accuracy and area coverage.

Powered by a diesel engine delivering output options ranging from 186 kW (250 HP) to 597 kW (800 HP).

Dimensions: 8 m × 6 m × 5 m.
