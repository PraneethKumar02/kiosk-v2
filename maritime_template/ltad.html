LEAPING TYPE AMPHIBIOUS DREDGER

Flow Rate

Up to 1000 cu.m./hr

Maximum Working Depth

Up to 6.7m

Draft

0.66 m

The system is developed through a technology collaboration with M/s Dragflow S.r.l, Italy, ensuring world-class dredging expertise.

It serves as an essential tool for dredging projects that require high mobility in both aquatic and terrestrial environments.

Its high-capacity pumps enable the handling of large material volumes, making it ideal for demanding dredging applications such as deep-water maintenance and mining projects.

It is suitable for a wide range of tasks, including river dredging, desilting, and land reclamation projects.

With multifunctional capabilities, leaping-type dredgers reduce operational costs by being adaptable to various project requirements.

It is powered by a diesel engine rated at 265 kW (360 HP) for reliable performance.

Dimensions: 10.5 m × 6.4 m × 3.4 m.
