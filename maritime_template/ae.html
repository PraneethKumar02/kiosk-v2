AMPHIBIAN EXCAVATOR

Operating Mass

23.7 tons

Bucket Capacity

0.3 cu.m.

Dredging Depth

3 - 4 m

The Amphibian Excavator is a compact, lightweight, and highly versatile machine.

It is specifically designed to operate in marshy, swampy, and waterlogged terrains where conventional excavators cannot function.

Its amphibious undercarriage enables seamless mobility over soft ground, shallow water, and wetland environments.

The design ensures exceptional stability and safety during operations, even in challenging terrain conditions.

The machine features dimensions of Boom 3.7m, Arm 1.6m, Pontoon (L 7.6m, B 1.4m, D 1.4m)
