CRAWLER TYPE AMPHIBIOUS DREDGER

Flow Rate

Up to 1000 cu.m./hr

Maximum Working Depth

Up to 6.5m

Draft

1.5 m

The system is developed through a technology collaboration with M/s Dragflow S.r.l, Italy, ensuring world-class dredging expertise.

Equipped with tracks for smooth and reliable movement on both solid and muddy terrain.

Its amphibious design enables crawler-type dredgers to operate in swampy areas, marshlands, and low-lying regions where conventional dredgers cannot reach.

It is suitable for a wide range of applications, from river dredging to land reclamation projects.

The machine supports environmental restoration by cleaning water bodies and aids in the management of wetlands and flood-prone areas.

It is powered by a diesel engine rated at 265 kW (360 HP) for reliable performance.

Dimensions: 11.5 m × 7 m × 4.5 m.
