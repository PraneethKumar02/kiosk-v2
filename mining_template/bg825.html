BG825 - Motor Grader

The BG825 is a heavy-duty motor grader built for demanding construction and mining operations. It combines high productivity with smooth and precise blade control. The operator benefits from a comfortable and well-positioned cabin for improved visibility. With monitoring and control systems that enhance safety and efficiency, it delivers reliable results in rough work environments.

Gross Power

280 HP

Blade Length

4928 mm

Operating Weight

28000 kg

The BG825 uses a high-power BEML engine, high-performance blade equipment and frame articulation to ensure high productivity. The electronic display monitoring system supports quick daily maintenance checks. It also alerts the operator in the event of any malfunction.

Operator comfort is ensured through an adjustable oil suspension seat and rubber-pad mounted operator’s compartment. A rear-frame mounted cabin enables greater visibility of the machine’s travel direction. Electrically controlled transmission with fingertip control offers smooth operation and maneuverability.

The machine provides 209 kW (280 HP) @ 2100 rpm, a blade length of 4,928 mm, and operating weight of 28,000 kg. These specifications make it suitable for heavy-duty road construction and mining-yard maintenance. It delivers strong grading force with stable and reliable performance.

High engine horsepower supports demanding work environments. The monitoring system, ergonomic cabin design and controls enhance daily operating efficiency. The BG825 is engineered to maintain productivity across rugged and challenging job sites.
