BE700 - HYDRAULIC EXCAVATOR

The BE700 is a fuel-efficient mining excavator designed for stable performance and productive digging operations. Its advanced hydraulics provide smooth movement and efficient control of all working functions. The operator cabin offers a comfortable workspace with excellent visibility for precise operation. With reliable structural components and easy service access, the BE700 delivers long-term durability and uptime in demanding working environments.

Power(Flywheel and Loading Shovel)

415 hp

Bucket capacity(Flywheel)

4.0 m³

Bucket capacity(Loading Shovel)

4.5 m³

The BE700 features simple and feather-touch work-equipment controls due to its hydraulically assisted control system. In Backhoe configuration, it delivers 309 kW (415 HP) @ 1800 rpm, 69,500 kg operating mass, and 4.0 m³ bucket capacity. The newly designed operator compartment offers ample workspace and excellent visibility.

In Loading Shovel / Face Shovel configuration, it provides 309 kW (415 HP) @ 1800 rpm, 70,000 kg operating mass, and 4.5 m³ standard bucket capacity. The BEML BSA6D140-1 diesel engine uses a direct injection combustion system for improved fuel economy. The machine is engineered as a fuel-efficient choice for heavy operations.

Three operating modes support different duty cycles: Electronic Standard and Medium-Duty modes (S & L) for high excavation efficiency and Manual mode (M) for light-duty work. The swing holding brake provides smooth lifting on inclines. Wide working range and a large undercarriage assure stable operation for maximum working performance.

The three-pump hydraulic system with independently controlled axial piston pumps ensures smooth compound movements and shorter cycle times. Positive control closed-loop hydraulics reduce losses and increase efficiency. Centralized monitoring in the cabin allows quick checks and alerts to abnormalities, enhancing reliability and service access.
