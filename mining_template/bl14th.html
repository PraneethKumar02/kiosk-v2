BL14TH - TYRE HANDLER

The BL14TH is a compact and highly capable tyre handler designed for efficient maintenance of large mining tyres. Its advanced steering and traction features allow reliable operation on uneven work surfaces. The hydraulic system and rotating head offer smooth and accurate control during tyre positioning. With excellent visibility and ergonomic control layout, it ensures safe and productive field operations.

Gross Power

148 HP

Flywheel Power

126 HP

Tyre Handling Force

1,91,334 N

The BL14TH is powered by a sturdy direct-injection turbocharged BEML diesel engine for sustained power and fuel economy. It provides 110 kW (148 HP) rated power, 94 kW (126 HP) flywheel power @ 2000 rpm, and 565 Nm max torque @ 1400 rpm. Closed-center load-sensing system (CLSS) with pilot hydraulic controls ensures smooth and accurate work-equipment operation.

The tyre handler attachment delivers up to 1,91,334 N gripping force with 200 mm lateral carriage movement for precision alignment. Smooth rotation of the head assembly supports safe and efficient tyre handling. It is engineered for a complete range of off-the-road large tyre maintenance operations.

Articulated power steering ensures higher maneuverability and faster cycles. Limited slip differential in front and rear axles provides positive traction while loading. Rear axle oscillation enhances stability on uneven and rough terrains.

Center-point articulation enables safer steering control during tight movements. All-wheel drive improves traction and ground engagement in mining environments. The BL14TH provides optimum operator control and visibility to enhance productivity and safety.
