ELECTRIC PERFORMANCE

The BH100 is a powerful 100-ton class mining dump truck built for reliable performance in tough haulage conditions. Driven by a robust 1082 HP Cummins QST30 engine and a rugged BEML power-shift transmission, it delivers strong tractive effort and smooth operation. With high payload capacity, advanced safety systems and a comfortable operator cabin, the BH100 ensures efficient and dependable mining productivity.

Safety & Braking

ROPS + Wet Disc Brakes

The BH100 is powered by a high-performance Cummins QST30 engine delivering 1082 HP, paired with a rugged BEML power-shift transmission and heavy-duty planetary axles for strong tractive effort and reliable operation. This powertrain ensures smooth performance even in demanding hauling conditions.

With a payload of 91,000 kg and a 60 m³ body capacity, the truck is built for efficient material transport. A loading indication system ensures proper payload by limiting body volume to 30 m³ for materials of 3-tonne/m³ density. Wet multiple disc brakes on both axles offer long service life and consistent braking.

The spacious, ergonomically designed cabin provides operator comfort with well-placed controls and an adjustable seat. Safety features include a ROPS cabin, emergency power steering, dual brake system and auto-retarder, ensuring safe operation in mining environments.

Orbitrol power steering delivers excellent maneuverability with an optimized turning circle and steering geometry. With low maintainability and repairability index (SAE-J817A) and strong BEML customer support, the BH100 offers dependable performance with reduced downtime.
