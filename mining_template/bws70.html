BWS70 - Water Sprinkler

The BWS70 is a heavy-duty water sprinkler designed for efficient dust suppression in mining and large-scale earthwork environments. It features a comfortable operator cabin with easy-to-use controls for smooth operation. High maneuverability allows the machine to perform effectively across rough and busy sites. With strong braking, steering, and safety systems, it delivers reliable performance for continuous site operations.

Flywheel Power

897 HP

Capacity

70000 Litres

Operating Mass

64000 kg

The BWS70 uses a high-power engine matched with a mechanical transmission to offer ample reserve power and a high HP-to-weight ratio. High power to weight ensures faster cycle time and improved productivity. Larger tank capacity further supports high-output operations.

The machine delivers 669 kW (897 HP) @ 2100 rpm and 70,000-litre capacity for effective dust suppression. Water discharge through sprinkling nozzles is assisted by a hydraulic motor and centrifugal pump for wider coverage. This ensures efficient dust settling in mining and construction zones.

Oil-cooled multiple disc rear brakes prevent fading even under repeated and continuous braking. The rear brakes also act as retarder brakes due to their ample capacity. Low maintainability and repairability index increases machine availability and simplifies servicing..

Human-engineered cabin ensures operator comfort with ergonomically designed compartment and controls. Orbitrol full-time power steering ensures high maneuverability through optimized steering geometry. Features include integral ROPS cabin, emergency steering and dual braking system for relevant safety compliance.
