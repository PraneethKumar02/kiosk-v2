CM20H - C-CRANE

The CM20H is a high-performance crane carrier designed for precise material handling in demanding environments. It ensures safe and comfortable operation through its advanced cabin and integrated monitoring systems. Smart drivetrain and hydraulic technologies enhance productivity while reducing operator effort. With strong stability and safety systems, it supports efficient loading operations in mining and industrial sites.

Gross Power

261 kW

Maximum Torque

1313 Nm

Engine

BEML EG

The CM20H is powered by a sturdy turbocharged after-cooled BEML EG diesel engine for sustained power and fuel economy. The automatic transmission with a perfectly matched torque converter eliminates operator error and adjusts automatically to varying loads. It delivers 261 kW (350 bhp) @ 2100 rpm and 1313 Nm torque @ 1400 rpm for efficient crane operations.

A sophisticated load-sensing hydraulic system ensures precise material handling. Increased crane speed during no-load operation contributes to faster cycle times. Wider wheelbase provides better stability while loading and maneuvering.

The front axle is equipped with wet multiple disc brakes, while the rear axle uses dry caliper disc brakes. An integral ROPS/FOPS cabin with AC enhances operator comfort and protection. Parking brake and emergency brake systems further improve operational safety.

Advanced safety and monitoring systems include rear vision camera, seat belt warning, auto fire suppression, operator fatigue monitoring system, and GPS (Telematics). Auto lubrication system improves uptime by reducing manual maintenance. The CM20H ensures reliable performance with enhanced serviceability and control.
