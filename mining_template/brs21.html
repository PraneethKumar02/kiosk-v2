ELECTRIC PERFORMANCE

The BEML BRS21 is a fully electric rope shovel engineered for high-production mining with zero emissions. Its rugged build and advanced diagnostics ensure reliable, stable, and efficient performance in the toughest conditions.

Operating Weight

720,000 kg

Dipper Capacity

21 Cu.m

Electric Drive System

IGBT + AFE

The BEML BRS21 is a fully electric rope shovel powered by an IGBT-based AC electrical drive system incorporating Active Front-End (AFE) technology for smooth, energy-efficient, and highly reliable operations. Equipped with rugged and reliable induction motors and proven IGBT power modules, the machine ensures stable performance even in tough mining environments. Its higher operating weight enhances overall stability during digging and loading, while the robust lower frame, independent propel system, and high-impact-resistant boom structure contribute to long-term durability. The aesthetic, spacious, and ergonomically designed operator cabin provides superior comfort, safety, and noise-free working conditions during extended shifts.

The BRS21 features a 21 Cu.m dipper capacity and an operating weight of 720,000 kg, enabling it to meet the production demands of large-scale mining operations. Its structural components are designed using advanced design tools that ensure optimal material selection, reduced stress concentrations, and extended structural life. The strong build quality and higher machine mass support increased dipper capacities, resulting in improved stability and overall productivity. These design considerations enable the equipment to perform reliably under heavy-duty mining conditions with consistent output.

Versatility is a key advantage of the BRS21, as it can be matched with various haul truck sizes depending on mine planning and production needs. The machine incorporates a rack-and-pinion system that provides positive crowd force for efficient digging and loading performance. Mechanical control of dipper door opening and closing ensures robust and dependable operation during continuous loading cycles. Additionally, the shovel supports zero-emission operation due to its fully electric architecture, aligning with modern sustainability goals and reducing environmental impact across mining sites.

The BRS21 integrates advanced automation and health monitoring technologies to maximize uptime and reduce maintenance-related delays. It features an automatic lubrication system with pneumatically operated pumps that ensure precise, timely lubrication to minimize wear and downtime. Embedded sensors, diagnostic tools, and PLC-based monitoring software enable early fault detection and preventive maintenance planning. With onboard and remote productivity monitoring, OITDS interface for seamless data access, and DGMS-compliant FRHR cables and safety components, the machine ensures high reliability, safety, and operational continuity in harsh mining environments.
