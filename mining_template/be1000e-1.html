BE1000E-1 - HYDRAULIC EXCAVATOR

The BE1000E-1 is an electrically driven mining excavator designed for efficient digging and loading operations. Its stable undercarriage and advanced hydraulics ensure smooth performance with faster cycles. The operator cabin offers great visibility, workspace, and easy control access. Safety and monitoring systems enhance reliability and protect both the operator and the machine in demanding environments.

Power(BACKHOE and LOADING SHOVEL)

400 kW

Bucket Capacity(BACKHOE)

3.1-5.55 m³

Bucket Capacity(LOADING SHOVEL)

4.5-7 m³

The BE1000E-1 features a wide working range and large undercarriage that ensure stable operation for maximum working performance. In Backhoe configuration, it uses a 400 kW electric motor, has an operating mass of 95,000 kg, and a bucket capacity of 3.1–5.55 m³. The specially designed cab provides ample workspace and excellent visibility.

In Loading Shovel / Face Shovel configuration, the machine maintains a 400 kW motor with an operating mass of 98,000 kg and a bucket capacity of 4.5–7.0 m³. A three-pump hydraulic system with independent closed-loop swing enables smooth compound movements. This system also shortens cycle time and supports efficient operation.

A powerful and economical 3.3 kV or 6.6 kV, 4-pole, 3-phase, 50 Hz TEFV squirrel-cage induction motor delivers dependable performance. Hydraulically assisted controls provide simple, light-touch operation for all work equipment. The wide undercarriage enhances stability during tough digging and loading tasks.

A centralized monitoring system enables quick daily checks and alerts the operator to abnormalities during operation. Automatic fire detection and suppression systems prevent accidents and reduce potential losses. The spacious cab supports comfortable and safe operation for extended working hours.
