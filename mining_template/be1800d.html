BE1800D - HYDRAULIC EXCAVATOR

The BE1800D is a high-capacity mining excavator engineered for tough and large-scale material handling tasks. It features a stable undercarriage and smooth hydraulic control for efficient digging and loading. The operator cabin offers excellent visibility and simple control access for productive operation. With automated lubrication, monitoring, and fire-safety systems, it ensures dependable performance in demanding environments.

Bucket Capacity(BACKHOE)

10 – 12 m³

Bucket Capacity(LOADING SHOVEL)

7.5-10.2 m³

Operating MASS

175000 kg

The BE1800D provides a wide working range and a large undercarriage to ensure stable operation and maximum working performance. In Backhoe configuration, it delivers 2 × 354 kW (2 × 475 HP) @ 2000 rpm, an operating mass of 1,75,000 kg, and a bucket capacity of 10 – 12 m³. A specially designed cab ensures ample workspace and excellent visibility.

In Face Shovel configuration, it maintains the same power and operating mass, with bucket capacity ranging from 7.5 – 10.2 m³. The four-pump hydraulic system with independent closed-loop swing ensures smooth compound movements and shorter cycle times. Hydraulically assisted controls provide simple, light-touch operation.

Two BEML turbocharged diesel engines ensure reduced fuel consumption and high operational economy. The turbocharger supports strong performance even at high-altitude job sites. Stability and productivity are enhanced by the robust undercarriage and working range.

A centralized monitoring system inside the cab keeps the operator informed and enables quick daily checks. Automatic lubrication systems reduce downtime by supplying grease to critical hinge pins and bearings. An automatic fire detection and suppression system minimizes risk and protects valuable resources.
