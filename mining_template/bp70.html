BP70 - PIPE LAYER

The BP70 is a reliable pipe-laying machine built to deliver stable lifting performance on pipeline projects. Its responsive controls and strong traction system enable precise positioning of pipes on uneven terrain. The machine is engineered with a rugged boom and durable undercarriage components for long service life. Easy maintenance and operator-friendly handling ensure efficient operations in demanding field conditions.

Gross Power

324 HP

Operating Mass

37000 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D170-1

The BP70 is powered by the BEML BS6D170-1 diesel engine, offering a generous HP-to-weight ratio for wide-range pipe-laying operations. Smooth and responsive power-shift transmission with single-lever control supports instant speed and directional changes. It delivers 242 kW (324 HP) @ 2000 rpm (ISO-9249) with an operating mass of 37,000 kg.

Maximum lifting capacity is provided as per the lifting performance curve to support various pipe-laying requirements. Joystick control enables precise hook movement during lifting operations. Interconnected wet-type steering clutches and brakes ensure easy control and long service life

The boom is a welded box-section structure operated by wire cables for high rigidity. Track-roller frames are made from high-tensile steel for structural strength. Sprockets with bolt-on rim segments permit quick on-site replacement without track removal.

Grooved floating seals in idlers, track and carrier rollers keep dirt out and lubrication inside. This design extends undercarriage life and reduces maintenance costs. The BP70 is engineered to provide reliable, stable performance in demanding pipeline environments.
