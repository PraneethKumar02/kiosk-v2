BG1205 - Motor Grader

The BG1205 is a high-performance motor grader designed for powerful and efficient grading operations. Its electronically controlled engine and smart transmission integration improve responsiveness and working smoothness. The operator benefits from joystick-based control and a hydraulically assisted braking system. With a focus on reliability, traction and control, it delivers safe and productive operation across rugged worksites.

Gross Power

550 HP

Engine

Cummins engine

Transmission

DANA Transmission

The BG1205 Motor Grader is powered by a 550 HP Cummins engine and deploys a DANA transmission. It features an Electronic Engine meeting Tier-3 emission norms with 550 HP gross power. The Electronic Power Shift Transmission provides 8 forward and 4 reverse speeds for versatile grading operations.

Wet disc brakes with accumulator-assisted hydraulic brake system ensure reliable stopping and enhanced safety. An in-bound parking brake on the tandem is assisted with an accumulator mounted on the tandem axle. These features together support safe and controlled ground engagement.

A variable piston pump with piggy-back pumps contributes to efficient hydraulic power distribution. Electronic Joystick control provides smooth and precise operation of work attachments. The hydrostatic fan drive improves power ratio and operational efficiency.

Engine and transmission electronic integration ensures optimum equipment performance. The hydrostatic fan drive is again emphasized for better power utilization under varying loads. The overall system design focuses on delivering controlled, powerful and efficient grading capability.
