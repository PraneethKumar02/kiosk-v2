BD355 - CRAWLER DOZER

The BD355 is a heavy-duty dozer engineered for powerful dozing in demanding mine conditions. It provides excellent control, stability, and traction with rugged structural design. The operator cabin enhances comfort and visibility for long working hours. With durable components and reduced-maintenance features, it ensures high productivity and long service life on tough job sites.

Flywheel Power

310 kW

Operating Mass

48660 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D170-1

The BD355 is powered by the BEML BS6D170-1 diesel engine which delivers a generous power-to-weight ratio for dynamic dozing performance. Smooth and responsive power-shift transmission with single-lever control ensures instant speed and directional changes. It has 310 kW (416 FHP) @ 2000 rpm and 48,660 kg operating mass with Semi-U dozer and ROPS cabin.

Conveniently located armchair steering control enhances operator comfort within a ROPS-FOPS hexagonal sound-suppressed cabin. Pilot-operated hydraulics enable effortless blade control through a joystick. Hydraulically interlinked steering clutches and brakes ensure excellent maneuverability during gradual and pivot turns.

The patented engine speed control mechanism through braking improves machine control and reduces brake band wear and oil contamination. Track-roller frames are built from high-tensile steel for maximum rigidity. High-tensile abrasion-resistant blades in multiple configurations cater to different applications and harsh ground conditions.

Bolt-on segmented sprockets allow quick on-site replacement and reduced service downtime. Groove-type floating seals on idlers, track and carrier rollers extend undercarriage life by retaining lubrication and blocking dirt. Engine, radiator, fuel tank, floor frame and cabin are mounted on anti-vibration rubber cushions to reduce noise and vibration.
