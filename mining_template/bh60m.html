BH60M - DUMP TRUCK

The BH60M is a reliable mining dump truck designed for efficient hauling with strong tractive effort and smooth operation. It uses an ALLISON automatic power-shift transmission with advanced control features for better performance under variable load conditions. The spacious and ergonomically designed cabin ensures operator comfort and ease of use. With built-in safety systems like a ROPS cabin, emergency steering, and sealed oil-cooled disc brakes, the BH60M delivers safe and dependable operations in mining environments.

Maximum Payload

54500 kg

Body Capacity

Flywheel Power

The BH60M dump truck is equipped with a high-power engine delivering 645 HP @ 2000 rpm, paired with an ALLISON automatic power-shift transmission and Electronic Control System (ECS). The planetary drive axle ensures the right balance of speed and tractive effort for demanding haul cycles. The torque converter maintains steady engine RPM under load, preventing engine stalling and improving efficiency.

With a maximum payload of 54,500 kg and a 36.4 m³ rock body capacity, it is designed for effective material hauling in mines and quarries. Sealed oil-cooled disc brakes offer reliable braking performance and long service life. The driveline is built for durability and smooth performance on challenging terrains.

The BH60M features a well-positioned, spacious and ergonomic cabin, ensuring operator comfort and reduced fatigue. Controls are placed for ease of operation and visibility is optimized for safer driving. A ROPS-certified cabin, along with emergency steering, enhances safety during critical operations.

Maintenance is simplified through a unitized design and easily accessible components, reducing downtime and repair effort. Orbitrol full-time power steering and a short turning radius ensure excellent maneuverability in confined mining environments. Overall, the BH60M delivers reliable, productive and safe haulage performance.
