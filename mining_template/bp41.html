BP41 - PIPE LAYER

The BP41 is a compact and efficient pipe layer designed for reliable lifting and placement operations in pipeline projects. Its hydraulic control systems and optimized transmission provide smooth, responsive handling. Rugged structural components ensure stability and longevity in demanding work environments. With simplified servicing and operator-friendly features, it delivers consistent productivity in the field.

Gross Power

180 HP

Operating Mass

29870 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D125-1

The BP41 is powered by a BEML BS6D125-1 diesel engine, delivering sustained power and fuel economy with a hydraulically actuated oil-cooled main clutch. It comes with a heavy-duty constant-mesh transmission using helical gears for reliable performance. It delivers 134 kW (180 HP) @ 1850 rpm with an operating mass of 29,870 kg for pipe-laying applications.

Maximum lifting capacity is supported as per the lifting performance curve to handle a wide range of pipes with joystick control. The boom is a welded box-section operated by wire cables, and track-roller frames are made from high-tensile steel for rigidity. Part commonality with the BEML BD80 model ensures easy servicing and proven reliability.

Hydraulically actuated, self-adjusting oil-cooled steering clutches ensure long service life. Steering brakes are also oil-cooled for better durability. Counterweights are hydraulically actuated to provide increased stability during pipe handling.

Faster operation is supported with two speeds for raising and lowering operations. The automatic boom kick-out device enhances efficiency and safety in field operations. The BP41 is engineered for productivity and reliable pipe-laying in rugged conditions.
