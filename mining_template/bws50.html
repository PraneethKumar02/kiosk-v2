BWS50 - Water Sprinkler

The BWS50 is a powerful water sprinkler designed for dual roles of dust suppression and site firefighting. Its strong drive system and durable structure make it suitable for heavy mining and haul road operations. Operator-friendly water delivery options enhance versatility during working conditions. With reliable spraying and safety features, it supports clean, safe, and productive work environments.

The BWS50 is powered by a Cummins QSK19 TII / TIII engine delivering 644 HP net power for high-performance water sprinkling operations. It achieves a maximum machine speed of 50 km/h, enabling fast mobility within mining and construction sites. Tank weight is 17,000 kg with a capacity of 50,000 liters for extended dust suppression.

The machine has a net vehicle weight of 46,200 kg and a gross vehicle weight of 96,200 kg when fully loaded. A pump capacity of 1833 liters per minute ensures high-volume water discharge. The water system supports both dust control and emergency fire response.

A manually operated water cannon enhances firefighting support on mining benches and haul roads. Three fishtail sprinkler nozzles provide uniform water coverage for effective dust settling. A 30-meter hose reel supports ground-based firefighting and wash-down tasks when required.

The compact machine layout ensures stability during full-load movement. The strong drivetrain and braking systems support safe handling under various terrain conditions. Designed for productivity and multipurpose site requirements, it enhances both safety and working environment quality.
