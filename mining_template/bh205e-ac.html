BH205E-AC - Dump Truck

The BH205E-AC Dump Truck delivers smooth and reliable performance with its powerful diesel engine and GE electric-drive system. Its strong hauling capability and efficient design ensure high productivity in demanding mining conditions. A spacious cabin, advanced braking systems, and easy serviceability provide safety, comfort, and dependable operation.

Capacity (Heaped)

111 m³

Maximum Payload

186 MT

Gross Power

2300 hp

The machine is powered by a high-performance Cummins diesel engine paired with a GE electric-drive system, delivering smooth gradeability, strong acceleration, and a high power-to-weight ratio. With 2300 hp (1716 kW) at 1900 rpm, it ensures reliable traction and efficient performance in demanding mining conditions.

With a heaped capacity of 111 m³ and a maximum payload of 186 MT, the equipment is built for high productivity. Its large target loading area, low loading height, and durable diesel-electric power module help achieve faster cycles, longer component life, and high machine availability.

The spacious deluxe cabin enhances operator comfort with large tinted windows, clear visibility, and intuitive, easy-to-reach controls. Its ergonomic layout reduces fatigue and supports safer, more efficient operation during long shifts.

Safety and control are strengthened through emergency power steering, electrical dynamic retarding with blown grids, and hydraulic caliper disc brakes. A wide steering angle ensures a short turning radius, while the low maintainability index allows easier service and reduced downtime.
