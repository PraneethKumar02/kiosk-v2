BL40 - Wheel Loader

The BL40 is a powerful wheel loader designed for efficient loading operations in tough site conditions. Its advanced hydraulic and transmission systems provide smooth bucket control and fast cycle times. The ergonomically designed operator cabin ensures comfort, visibility, and precise maneuvering. With durable construction and smart monitoring, it delivers reliable performance with reduced maintenance effort.

Gross Power

300 HP

Operating Mass

28000 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D140-1

The BL40 is powered by a high-power BEML BS6D140-1 engine paired with a rugged electric-shift transmission offering 3 forward and 3 reverse speeds. A heavy-duty planetary axle provides strong tractive effort and gradeability in demanding conditions. Power-shift transmission ensures smooth and responsive shifting for instant speed and directional changes.

Pilot-operated hydraulic controls enable effortless fine bucket movement using a joystick. Z-bar linkages provide optimum breakout force and faster cycle times for efficient loading. Smooth Orbitrol steering improves maneuverability during both gradual and sharp turns, enhancing operator comfort.

Attention-free wet multiple disc brakes on front and rear axles ensure longer service life. Box-type chassis construction offers uniform stress distribution for durability. Sealed loader linkage pins improve performance while reducing maintenance.

A roomy walk-through cab with ergonomically arranged controls supports easy operation of forward and reverse gear selections. An air-suspension adjustable seat and excellent visibility ensure comfortable and accurate loading. Electronic display and monitoring systems help prevent minor issues from becoming major ones.
