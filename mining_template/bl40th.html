BL40TH - TYRE HANDLER

The BL40TH is a specialized tyre-handling machine designed for efficient servicing of large mining tyres. It provides powerful lifting performance with smooth and precise attachment control. The machine offers strong traction and excellent maneuverability on uneven terrain. With enhanced stability, operator visibility, and safety controls, it delivers reliable performance in demanding mine environments.

Gross Power

250 kW

Tyre Lifting Capacity

8000 kg

Operating Mass

32000 kg

The BL40TH features an electronic engine designed for sustained power and improved fuel economy. It offers 32,000 kg operating mass, 8,000 kg tyre lifting capacity, and 250 kW gross power @ 2100 rpm. The electro-hydraulic proportional control system ensures precise operation of the tyre-handler attachment.

A closed-center load-sensing system (CLSS) with pilot hydraulic controls improves working efficiency. Continuous rotation of gripping pads allows easy handling of large tyres. Smooth rotation of the head assembly supports controlled positioning during replacement work.

Articulated power steering provides higher maneuverability in tight mining environments. Heavy-duty front and rear axles offer positive traction on rugged terrain. Rear axle oscillation enhances machine stability on uneven ground.

Center-point articulation ensures safer steering operations with better control. A neutral starter switch prevents the engine from starting if the transmission is in gear. The BL40TH supports a complete range of large off-the-road tyre handling applications.
