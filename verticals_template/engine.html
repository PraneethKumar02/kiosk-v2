1500HP ENGINES FOR MAIN BATTLE TANKS

The BEML 1500HP DATRAN 1500 engine marks a major leap in India’s defense self-reliance, developed by BEML in collaboration with DRDO’s CVRDE and global partner Ricardo, and achieving an impressive 90% indigenous content. Engineered to power next-generation Main Battle Tanks such as the Arjun Mk-2, it will soon be integrated into an Arjun prototype for mobility trials, paving the way for full-scale production by late 2027.
