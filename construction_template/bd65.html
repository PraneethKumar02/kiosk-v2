BD65 - DOZER

The BD65 is a durable and efficient dozer built for stable traction and smooth earthmoving operations. Its ergonomic cabin design ensures better visibility and comfortable control for long working hours. The machine provides excellent handling with its power-shift transmission and responsive steering system. With rugged construction and extended-life components, it delivers reliable productivity in demanding terrain.

Gross Power

180 HP

Operating Mass

18830 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D125-1

The BD65 uses a BEML BS6D125-1 direct-injection diesel engine designed for high fuel efficiency and cleaner exhaust even at high altitudes. A power-shift transmission allows smooth shifting with instant speed and direction change using a single lever. It provides 134 kW (180 HP) flywheel power and an operating mass of 18,830 kg with standard angle dozer equipment.

Wet steering clutches and brakes ensure long service life and quick steering response. Arm-chair steering controls enhance operator ergonomics and comfort. Joystick-operated hydraulics provide effortless control for all blade functions.

A longer track frame delivers excellent machine balance, stability, and traction on soft terrains. Floating seals in idlers and rollers prevent dirt entry, increasing component durability. Dust seals between link, bushing, and pin ensure extended track life in harsh conditions.

The spacious operator cabin offers better visibility and safety along with ergonomically positioned controls. The adjustable operator seat enhances comfort during extended working hours. Simple maintenance features include spin-on filters, dust-protected air cleaner, and easily accessible control valves.
