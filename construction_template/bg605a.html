BG605A - MOTOR GRADER

The BG605A is a high-performance motor grader designed for tough road and mining applications. Its smooth hydro-shift transmission and efficient blade system ensure fast and accurate grading. The ergonomic cabin provides excellent visibility and operator comfort for long shifts. Rugged and powerful, it delivers reliable productivity in demanding job conditions.

Blade Length

3710 mm

Flywheel Power

121 kW

Engine

Bharat Stage III CEV

The BG605A is powered by a Bharat Stage III CEV compliant high-power engine that enhances productivity in demanding terrain. Hydro-shift transmission provides smooth operation with fingertip gear shifting for improved maneuverability. It features 121 kW flywheel power @ 1800 rpm and a 3,710 mm blade for strong grading performance.

The ergonomically designed operator cabin offers generous space and excellent visibility. Adjustable suspension seating enhances comfort during long working hours. The operator’s compartment is mounted on rubber pads to reduce vibration and fatigue.

Front-frame mounted cabin design ensures clear visibility of the machine’s travel direction. High-performance blade systems enable efficient cutting, leveling, and shaping of road surfaces. This makes the BG605A suitable for heavy-duty road construction and mining site maintenance.

The hydro-shift system supports smooth directional transitions and precise speed changes. Overall structure is engineered for stability, strength, and reliable performance in rugged job environments. The BG605A maintains productivity even in harsh operating conditions.
