BG405A - MOTOR GRADER

The BG405A is a modern motor grader designed for high-precision road and construction work. Its efficient Stage IV engine and smooth electronic transmission deliver strong and responsive grading performance. The cabin provides exceptional visibility and ergonomic comfort for long operations. With advanced controls and a wide working range, it ensures reliable productivity in all terrains

Gross Power

133 HP

Operating Mass

11,970 kg

Engine

Ashok Leyland H6

The BG405A is equipped with an Ashok Leyland H6 Series CEV Stage IV compliant engine that provides an optimum power-to-weight ratio. Power-shift electronic transmission offers smooth and efficient automatic or manual gear shifting. The articulated frame design allows a wide working range for versatile grading operations.

The operator cabin is ergonomically designed to provide spacious working space for improved comfort. High visibility enhances operational safety and accuracy during grading tasks. Rocker switches with clear legends improve usability and control.

A centralized compact electronic LCD gauge cluster keeps the operator informed of system status. Controls are positioned within easy reach for reduced fatigue. The machine layout prioritizes operator convenience and reliability.
