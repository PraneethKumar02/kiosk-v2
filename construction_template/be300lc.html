BE300LC - EXCAVATOR

The BE300LC is a high-capacity excavator built for demanding earthmoving and mining applications. It offers smooth hydraulic control and excellent precision during all types of digging and loading operations. The ergonomically designed cabin ensures comfort and efficient machine control. With advanced monitoring features and strong structural performance, it delivers reliable productivity in tough environments.

Gross Power

197 HP

Operating Mass

30800 kg

Bucket Capacity

0.5–2.0 m³

The BE300LC is powered by a BEML BS6D105-1 direct-injection diesel engine for high fuel efficiency and cleaner exhaust at high altitudes. Power-shift transmission allows smooth power shifting and instant directional changes with a single lever. It offers 147 kW (197 HP) @ 1550 rpm, 0.5–2.0 m³ bucket capacity (SAE Heaped), and 30,800 kg operating mass for heavy-duty excavation work.

Positive control double-pump hydraulic system ensures smooth independent and simultaneous equipment functions. Horsepower summation fully utilizes engine output to support faster cycle times and improved productivity. Load-sensing control valve and regeneration circuits reduce hydraulic loss during idling and ensure precise oil flow based on joystick movement.

A turbocharger enables high engine performance even at high-altitude job sites. Hydraulically assisted controls provide feather-touch handling of work attachments. Mode selection switch allows operators to choose the right mode for digging, loading, light lifting, or slope finishing applications.

A centralized monitoring system enables fast daily checks and alerts the operator of abnormalities during operation. The cabin is aesthetically improved with enhanced workspace and excellent visibility. The BE300LC is engineered for efficient, precise, and comfortable operation in demanding conditions.
