BG605I - MOTOR GRADER

The BG605I is a modern and powerful motor grader built for demanding road construction and maintenance work. Its advanced transmission and high-performance blade ensure efficient and accurate grading. The spacious operator cabin provides great visibility and comfort for long working hours. With stable design and enhanced maneuverability, it delivers strong productivity across tough job conditions.

Gross Power

108 kW

Operating Mass

14700 kg

Engine

Ashok Leyland H6 Series

The BG605I is equipped with an Ashok Leyland H6 Series CEV Stage V compliant engine. Power-shift electronic transmission supports both automatic and manual shifting for smooth operation. Its high engine horsepower and robust configuration make it ideal for heavy-duty road construction and maintenance applications.

The ergonomically designed operator cabin provides generous space and high visibility. Adjustable suspension seating enhances comfort during long work shifts. Rubber-pad mounting of the operator’s compartment helps reduce vibration and operator fatigue.

High engine power coupled with a high-performance blade results in improved productivity. Automatic and manual shifting enhances maneuverability in varied terrain conditions. The grader’s structure ensures reliable and stable operation in demanding environments

Rear-frame mounted operator’s station makes it easier to view the machine’s travel direction. A centralized compact electronic LCD gauge cluster gives real-time machine information. The BG605I is engineered for precision grading and long-duration productivity.
