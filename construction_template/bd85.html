BD85 - DOZER

The BD85 is a performance-driven dozer built to tackle challenging earthmoving and mining environments. It combines strong traction and precise blade control for efficient dozing operations. The cabin is designed for superior visibility and operator comfort. With rugged construction and smooth-operating systems, it consistently delivers reliable results in the field.

Gross Power

230 HP

Operating Mass

24340 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D125-1

The BD85 is powered by the BEML BS6D125-1 direct injection diesel engine for high fuel efficiency and cleaner exhaust at high altitudes. Power-shift transmission enables smooth shifting and instant directional changes using a single lever. It delivers 172 kW (230 HP) @ 2000 rpm with an operating mass of 24,340 kg equipped with a straight tilt dozer.

The operator cabin is spacious, ensuring better visibility and enhanced safety. Armchair steering control with patented cushion mounting improves operator comfort. Modular control panel with clustered gauges provides better monitoring during operation

Higher drawbar pull and increased speeds ensure enhanced productivity on all terrain types. Longer track frame improves traction and machine stability in demanding conditions. Foot-operated braking with hydraulic boost support ensures light-touch and reliable actuation.

Hydraulically interconnected brakes and steering clutches support precise steering control. High tensile steel blades in multiple configurations withstand harsh working environments. Rugged driveline components offer high durability and longer service life, while joystick pilot control provides effortless blade operation.
