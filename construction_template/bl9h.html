BL9H - BACKHOE LOADER

The BL9H is a versatile backhoe loader built for productivity in construction and utility work. Its efficient hydraulic and drivetrain systems allow smooth digging and loading operations. The operator-friendly cabin enhances visibility and comfort for long working hours. With strong durability and reduced maintenance needs, it performs reliably in demanding job conditions.

Gross Power

74 HP

Operating Mass

7700 kg

Engine

CEV Stage V direct-injection diesel engine

The BL9H is powered by a CEV Stage V direct-injection diesel engine for reduced fuel consumption and high operational economy. It delivers 55 kW (74 hp) @ 2200 rpm with an operating mass of 7,600 kg (4x2) or 7,700 kg (4x4). The turbocharger enables high performance even at high-altitude job sites.

Effortless power steering combined with optimized travel speed, wheelbase, and turning radius enhances maneuverability. Electronic display and monitoring system helps prevent minor problems from becoming major failures. Fully oscillating steerable front axle and rigid planetary rear axle ensure strong traction and stability.

Special dust seals on linkage joints improve durability and reduce maintenance frequency. Wet multiple disc brakes on rear axle wheels provide reliable braking during continuous operations. All structures are made from high-tensile steel for extended life.
