BD80 - DOZER

The BD80 is a rugged dozer designed for strong traction and heavy earthmoving operations. Its hydraulic control systems enable quick and smooth handling of every blade movement. The machine is built with reinforced structural elements to withstand continuous tough usage. With durable undercarriage components and efficient power delivery, it ensures dependable performance in challenging environments.

Gross Power

180 HP

Operating Mass

21550 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D125-1

The BD80 is powered by a BEML BS6D125-1 direct-injection diesel engine that ensures sustained power, high fuel efficiency, and cleaner exhaust at high altitudes. Wet-type main clutch is hydraulically boosted and hand-controlled for smooth and light-touch operation. It delivers 134 kW (180 HP) @ 1850 rpm with an operating mass of 21,550 kg using an angle dozer.

A sturdy box-type main frame construction withstands shocks and stresses during heavy dozing tasks. Direct drive system with constant-mesh helical gears provides reliable and efficient transmission performance. Hydraulically actuated main clutch and steering clutches enhance durability and responsiveness.

Oil-cooled steering clutches and brakes ensure long service life even in demanding operations. The hydraulically controlled dozer blade features a fully ribbed mould board for strength and productivity. Heavy-duty drivetrain and structural components improve machine stability and traction.

Floating seals in rollers, idlers, and sprockets prevent dirt intrusion and retain lubrication. Special dust seals protect track bushings and pins, ensuring extended track life. The BD80 is built for high reliability, easy handling, and long-term operation under tough site conditions.
