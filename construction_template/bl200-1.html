BL200-1 - WHEEL LOADER

The BL200-1 is a high-performance wheel loader powered by a CEV Stage V compliant Ashok Leyland turbo-charged engine, designed for efficient material handling. It delivers smooth operation with a powershift transmission and precise bucket control through a pilot-operated hydraulic system. The Orbitrol steering system enhances manoeuvrability even in tight working areas. Engine and transmission are vibration-isolated to ensure reliable performance and superior operator comfort in demanding job sites.

Power up to

857 ps

Acceleration (0–62 mph)

2.8 seconds

Maximum range

332–369 miles

The BL200-1 features a CEV Stage V Compliant H-Series Ashok Leyland Turbo-charged Engine for wide range of loading applications. Powershift transmission provides smooth and responsive shifts for instant speed and directional changes. Smooth Orbitrol steering ensures excellent manoeuvrability with enhanced operator comfort.

Pilot operated hydraulic system offers effortless fine control of bucket through joystick. The powerful engine combined with responsive driveline supports productivity in various work environments. Every component ensures smooth handling and improved work efficiency.

Engine and transmission are mounted on anti-vibration rubber cushions to isolate vibration and reduce noise levels. This provides better comfort and increases durability of major aggregates. The BL200-1 is designed for reliable performance in demanding construction applications.
