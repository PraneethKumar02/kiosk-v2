BD50 -DOZER

The BD50 is a compact dozer built for fuel-efficient operation in earthmoving and utility applications. Its smooth powertrain and responsive control system make dozing easier for the operator. The machine provides strong durability thanks to its rugged frame and protected undercarriage components. With low maintenance requirements, it ensures consistent performance in tough work environments.

Gross Power

90 HP

Operating Mass

12170 kg

Engine

BEML BS6D105-1

The BD50 is powered by a turbocharged, direct-injection BEML BS6D105-1 diesel engine for high fuel efficiency and cleaner exhaust. Wet-type main clutch is hydraulically boosted and foot-controlled for smooth and light-touch operation. It delivers 67 kW (90 HP) @ 1750 rpm and has an operating mass of 12,170 kg with an angle dozer.

A spur-gear selective sliding transmission with splash lubrication ensures reliable operation. The main frame and steering case are welded into a rigid monoblock construction for better stability. Unit construction of engine, clutch, and transmission assemblies ensures quicker servicing and overhaul.

Floating seals in oil-filled rollers, sprockets, and idlers extend undercarriage life. Reduced maintenance time and costs are ensured through reliable sealing and component protection. Dust seals between link bushing and pin prevent abrasive dirt entry and increase track life.
