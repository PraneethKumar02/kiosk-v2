BL30-1 - WHEEL LOADER

The BL30-1 is a powerful wheel loader built for productive material handling in construction and mining sites. Its efficient drivetrain and hydraulic system ensure fast cycle times and reliable digging force. The ergonomically designed cabin supports safe and comfortable operation throughout long shifts. With rugged durability and advanced control features, it delivers consistent performance in challenging conditions.

Gross power

222 HP

Operating Mass

18000 kg

Engine

CEV Stage IV Engine

The BL30-1 is powered by a CEV Stage IV compliant Ashok Leyland H-Series turbocharged engine suited for a wide range of loading applications. Power-shift transmission allows smooth and responsive shifting for instant speed and directional changes. Pilot-operated hydraulics provide effortless fine bucket control through a joystick.

A rugged transmission with 4 forward and 3 reverse speeds ensures strong tractive effort and gradeability in demanding conditions. Load-sensing hydraulic system improves work attachment operation efficiency. Wet multiple disc brakes on both axles and a box-type chassis ensure strength and uniform stress distribution.

A 7" electronic display and monitoring system assists with quick troubleshooting. Dust-sealed loader linkage pins reduce maintenance requirements. The ROPS & FOPS cabin includes ergonomic controls, cushioned adjustable seating, and excellent visibility for safe and easy material handling.

Z-bar linkage delivers optimum breakout force and faster cycle times for efficient loading. Articulated power steering improves maneuverability with optimal turning performance. The system balances travel speed, hydraulic responsiveness, and overall productivity.
