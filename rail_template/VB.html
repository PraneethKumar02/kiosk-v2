Vande Bharat

The train consists of 16 coaches, including 11 AC three-tier, four AC two-tier, and one AC first-class coach, providing a total of 823 berths. It is equipped with jerk-free semi-permanent couplers, anti-climbers, and a crash-worthy car-body design compliant with EN standards. The trainset can operate at 160 kmph and incorporates advanced safety features, including crash-worthy design elements.

The sleeper version is equipped with amenities such as integrated reading lights with USB charging, public announcement and visual information systems, modular pantries, special berths and toilets for differently-abled passengers, hot-water showers in the first AC coach, CCTV surveillance, automatic doors, cushioned berths, end wall fire barrier door, onboard Wi-Fi, and touch-free bio-vacuum toilets.

Passengers with restricted mobility (PRM) are provided special accommodation and accessible toilets in the driving coaches on both ends of the train.

An emergency talk-back unit connects passengers to the loco pilots in case of emergencies.

The train includes the Kavach anti-collision system, anti-climbing technology, and conforms to EN-45545 HL3 fire safety standards.

Feature Details
Technologies/features Involved - Austenitic Stainless steel carbody
- Crashworthiness standard of EN 15227, C1 Category
- Static Structural strength of carbody - P1 Category
- Fire safety as per EN 45545, Hazard Level: 03
- Aerodynamic exterior looks
- Best-in-class interiors with GFRP panels
- Light weight Aluminium Honeycomb partition walls and berths
- Public announcement and visual information system (PA/PIS with Emergency Talk back & Infotainment System)
