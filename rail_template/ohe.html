8 Wheeler OHE CAR

The vehicle is meant for periodic inspection, patrolling, and maintenance of overhead equipment on electrified broad-gauge routes and is used to attend breakdown sites. It has driver cabs at both ends, staff cabins, a kitchenette, a hydraulic lifting and swiveling platform, and an observation dome. It is capable of erecting and restoring small lengths of damaged catenary and contact wires and includes a roof-mounted pantograph for checking OHE parameters, along with an intercom facility between the driver’s cab and the lifting platform.

Max. Speed

110kmph

Power

2*213KW (2*285 hp) @ 2100 rpm
