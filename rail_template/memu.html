Mainline EMU

The system uses 945 V 3-phase AC for traction motors, 415 V 3-phase AC for the compressor and auxiliary circuits, and 110 V DC for lights, fans, and controls. It is equipped with 110 V, 120 Ah VRLA batteries rated for 10 hours to provide emergency lighting.

Max. Speed

120kmph

Feature Details
Axle Load 20.32 Tonnes (Motar Coach)
16.25 Tonnes (Trailer Coach)
Braking System Regenerative EP brake system interfaced with TCMS.
Propulsion System - Line supply voltage 25kV AC, single phase.
- Control equipment permits multiple operation of units.
- 3 phase propulsion system with microprocessor-controlled IGBT-based VVVF speed control of Traction motor.
- 4 nos. of 3 phase Traction motors of 347KW each per motor coach.
- Modular Transformer and Modular compressor.
Other Features - GPS based PA/PIS communication system.
- LED Lights for energy efficiency.
- Electrically operated wipers.
- Event Recorder for Continuous monitoring and automatic recording of train data.
