8W DETC Diesel Electric Tower Car

The vehicle has a dual power pack with an under-slung diesel engine and a transmission system consisting of a traction alternator with power rectifier and two traction motors per bogie (three-phase DC). It uses a CBC coupler per RDSO spec 56-BD-07 and a panel-mounted air brake system. It is meant for periodic inspection, patrolling, and maintenance of overhead equipment on electrified broad-gauge tracks, attending breakdown sites, and erecting or restoring small lengths of catenary and contact wires.
