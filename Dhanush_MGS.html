HMV 8x8 for DHANUSH Mounted Gun System

BEML HMV 8x8 is designed to mount the indigenously developed 155 mm/52 Cal howitzer and includes a strengthened chassis with a dedicated gun-carrying platform and deployable outriggers for stable firing.

The Customized vehicle chassis provides the vehicle an exceptional resistance to shocks & vibrations, protects superstructure from torsion & stresses.

High ride quality reduced vibrations and road shocks provide improved driver comfort and safety for transport of sensitive and special goods, equipment and troops.
