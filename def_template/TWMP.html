Track Width Mine Plough(TWMP) T-90

T-90 TWMP (Track Width Mine Plough) has been developed to meet the demands of the modern Main Battle Tank (MBT).

The TWMP gives a tank the ability to rapidly force a passage through mined obstacles by creating a cleared path for the T-90 Main Battle Tank.

Clears mines in front of the track width of the vehicle (tread way). Designed to operate in a wide range of soils including loose sand and loams.

The TWMP is designed to minimize the traction effort and minimize the effects of detonating mines and requires minimal maintenance.
