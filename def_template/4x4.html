BEML High Mobility Vehicle (HMV) 4x4 is built to operate in rough terrain, challenging climates, and extreme environmental conditions, making it highly suitable for expeditionary forces.

The 4x4 all-wheel drive chassis employs new development of the Independent suspension and backbone tube frame, which are unique characteristics.

These characteristics allows each wheel to move independently, with improved steering, and maximum tyre-to-ground contact, while featuring extreme resistance of the chassis against torsion and bending
