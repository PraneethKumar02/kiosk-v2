Heavy Recovery Vehicle 8x8

Crane Lifting Capacity

20t at 2m

Winch Pull

15t with 150m

Dozer light digging Depth

Upto 250mm

BEML HRV 8x8 vehicle is designed to recover stuck-up, buried or overturned causalities of armoured or engineering vehicles.

This has a recovery crane with lifting capacity of 20t @ 2m radius with Hydraulic stabilizers and light dazing upto 250mm with dozer attachment. The recovery winch has a pull of 15t for effective length of 150m.

This vehicle is provided with all wheel drive for maximum traction, swinging half axles for better cross country mobility, centralized tyre inflation, deflation system, self recovery from front and rear.

It can tow rigid and suspended load upto 11t, tow trailers upto 15t in cross country and 65t on highways.
