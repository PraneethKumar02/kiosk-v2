Track Width Mine Plough Arjun MBT

The Track Width Mine Plough (TWMP) gives the Arjun Main Battle Tank (MBT) the ability to rapidly force a passage through mine field by creating a cleared path.

It rapidly clears mines from the track width of the host vehicle and designed to minimize the effect of detonating mines.

It is Designed to minimize the traction effort required and also Capable of operating in a wide range of soil conditions.
