HMV 8x8 with Armoured Cabin

EML & DRDO (VRDE) designed HMV 8x8 for Mounted Gun System application.Strengthened, low height Armoured Cabin is designed to withstand blast Pressure & Temperature of exhaust gases from muzzle.

The cabin has protection against small arms as well. Cabin is equipped with powerful HVAC and seating provisions for six crew members.The vehicle chassis provides an exceptional resistance to shocks & vibrations, protects superstructure from torsion & stresses.

High ride quality-reduced vibrations & road shocks provide improved driver comfort and safety for transport of sensitive special goods, equipment and troops.

An auxiliary cabin has been provided on the chassis to accommodate single crew and the chassis is strengthened for MGS role.
