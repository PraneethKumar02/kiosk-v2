ARJUN Armoured Recovery & Repair Vehicle

The system provides comprehensive field recovery and repair of MBTs, including hull repairs using built-in welding and cutting facilities. It carries a complete set of tools for immediate repairs and supports assembly and disassembly of major aggregates on-site. Additionally, it is capable of recovering bogged vehicles in difficult terrain and is equipped with a remote-controlled air-defense weapon system for protection during operations.
