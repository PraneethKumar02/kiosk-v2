Tank Transporter HMV 8x8

BEML HMV 8x8 with 4 door cabin is an all terrain vehicle mainly used for tank transporter role in Indian Army. The vehicle is also used for transport of personnel & platform for keeping dummy weight while towing trailer for better road traction, is having a payload capacity of more than 11t.

The vehicle can tow trailers on public roads up to 100t and on terrain conditions trailer weighing up to 25t. The vehicle is provided with a winch for self recovery & for loading of dead tanks on to the trailer. The vehicle is provided with centralized tyre inflation/deflation system inside the cabin which enables inflation/deflation of tyres depending on terrain conditions.
