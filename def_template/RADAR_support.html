Radar Support Vehicles

High Mobility Vehicle HMV 8x8 is designed as a common base vehicle authorised for reconnaissance & support battalions of mechanized infantry for controlling the surveillance grid, collection of surveillance inputs from radars, collation of data & its dissemination.

As these vehicles operates with mechanized forces & therefore combines the operational requisites of mobility, protection & communication.

Radars mounted on HMV 8x8 vehicle platform are:
- Weapon Locating Radar (WLR)-"SWATHI"
- 3D Tactical Control Radar (3DTCR)
- Air Defence Fire Control Radar (ADFCR)
- Indian Doppler RADAR - "INDRA"
- Low Level Transportable Radar (LLTR)
- Air Defence Tactical Control Radar (ADTCR)
- "ASHWINI" Radar
- "ROHINI" give in points
