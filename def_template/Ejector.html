EJECTOR AIR & CLEANER ASSEMBLY

Ejector & Air Cleaner assembly has been Indigenously developed for an Infantry Combat Vehicle BMP-II tank and made up of special aluminium alloy, Ejector will ejects the exhaust gases from the engine, generate smoke screen & cools the radiator.

Air cleaner system separates the dust particles from the Air fed to the Engine by centrifugal action through cyclone tubes.
