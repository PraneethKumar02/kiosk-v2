20 TON TRAILER

This 20t Capacity low deck trailer used for transportation of light duty tanks, dozers (BD 80, BD 50) & wheeled equipment.

The front portion of the trailer is provided with turntable which is steerable to 68° both sides, twin line air brake system with ABS, emergency brakes and lever/knob operated parking brakes.
