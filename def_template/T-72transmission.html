Tank T-72 Transmission

The T-72 transmission consists of two planetary gearboxes LH & RH (each gearbox is structurally united with a planetary final drive).

The gearbox provides 7 forward gears and 1 reverse gear. The gearbox is fitted with mechanical brake drives and friction clutches with metal-and-ceramic discs that feature enhanced wear resistance.

The gearbox is installed in the housing available in the tank. Transmission overall weight is 1765 kg & Dimensions: φ635 mm & L-920 mm, having a Maximum transmitted power of 620 kW.
