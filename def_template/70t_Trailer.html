70 Ton Trailer

70t Trailer is a 40 wheeled heavy duty full trailer designed to go in rough terrains with a payload capacity of 70t.

The trailer is used for transporting battle tanks, tracked vehicles, earthmoving and other heavy equipment.

The trailer is having advanced hydraulic suspension with provision of lifting individual axles, stearing of 4 rows of axles. Dual brake system, emergency break & knob operated parking brakes.
