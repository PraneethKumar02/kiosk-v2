MIlitary & Disaster Relief

The BEML HMV 6x6 is used by the Indian Army for general service roles and is designed as an all-terrain personnel transport vehicle

It can tow trailers up to 65 tonnes on public roads and 16 tonnes on cross-country terrain, supported by planetary wheel-hub reductions and independent wheel suspension with swinging semi-axles.

The vehicle uses leaf springs on the rear axles and torsion bars with telescopic shock absorbers on the front axle, and it can be customised for a variety of applications.
