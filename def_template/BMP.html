BMP Transmission

Mechanical Transmission with steering system has been Indigenously developed for an Infantry Combat Vehicle BMP -II tank, it is a critical power line aggregate of a tank with input power of 300 hp at 2600 rpm.

Shifting of gears mechanically with constant mesh gear type. BEML has supplied these transmission to AVNL and Indian Army, also providing support for Spares & Overhauling.
