SARVATRA BRIDGING SYSTEM

The SARVATRA system is mounted on an indigenously manufactured BEML High Mobility Vehicle (HMV) 8x8 and provides a versatile combat assault bridging solution for mechanized forces. It is a multi-span mobile bridging system with individual spans of 15 meters, allowing a single set to bridge wet or dry obstacles up to 75 meters span in steps of 15 meters. Designed for Military Load Classification (MLC-70), SARVATRA features a double “A”-shaped trestle for exceptional stability and advanced hydraulics for smooth, continuous operation. In addition to military applications, it can also serve as an emergency bridge during natural disasters such as floods and earthquakes. The Sarvatra bridge system can be customized to meet specific operational requirements.
