MIlitary & Disaster Relief

The SARVATRA system is mounted on an indigenously manufactured BEML High Mobility Vehicle (HMV) 8x8 and provides a versatile combat assault bridging solution for mechanized forces.

Designed for Military Load Classification (MLC-70), SARVATRA features a double “A”-shaped trestle that ensures exceptional stability along with advanced hydraulics that enable smooth, continuous deployment and retrieval.

The Sarvatra bridge system can be further customized to meet mission-specific and terrain-specific operational requirements.
