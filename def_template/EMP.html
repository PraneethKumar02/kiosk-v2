Engineering Mine Plough T-90

T-90 Engineer Mine Plough gives tank the ability to rapidly force a passage through mined obstacles by creating a cleared path for armored fighting vehicles to follow.

The mine free path is prepared by a raking action that brings buried or concealed mines to the surface and moves them to the side.

Clears mines in front of the track of host vehicle and also Clears mines buried at certain depths. Can operate at temperatures ranging from -20 deg C to +55 deg C.

Heavy duty jacks are mounted on EMP facilities for easy fitting and removal. No crane is required for fitting and removals.
